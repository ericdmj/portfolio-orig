Urban Areas and Access to Public Lands

These were illustrations from a report about public lands and accessibility in select urban areas. The term PPA in the details below refers to the number of public protected areas--parks (local, state, and national) and other outdoor recreation lands--that intersect with the urban area in question. The prime idea was to clearly show each urban area, its public lands, and its transit points.
