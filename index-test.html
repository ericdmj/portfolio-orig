Eric D. M. Johnson

Maps for museums,
researchers, & more

From the map of Hershey Park I put on my wall before I was five years old to deep dives into primary-source maps in historical archives to detailed work with geographic information systems and digital cartography, the power of maps to communicate place has been endlessly fascinating to me.

This site captures a little of that fascination and that work.
